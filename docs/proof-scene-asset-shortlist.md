## Accepted import rationale

Kenney packs were selected for the first proof sample because their pages and bundled license files identify Creative Commons CC0 licensing, they provide direct text OBJ files, require no account credentials, and can be integrated in Godot without Blender.

## Rejected or deferred rationale

Quaternius packs are promising, but the pivot imported only a minimal clearly verified sample. Packs with purchase/source-tier ambiguity or unnecessary breadth were deferred until human review confirms whether the Kenney proof direction is worth extending.
