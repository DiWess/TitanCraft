# TitanCraft Shape Language

## Human Technology

Human technology must read as rectangular, modular, practical, industrial, manufactured, and repaired repeatedly.

### Allowed Human Forms

- rectangular hull panels with thickness and bevels;
- modular crates, ribs, brackets, rails, removable panels, hatches, vents, and repair plates;
- cylinders only when mechanically justified, such as hinges, pistons, tanks, cables, hoses, conduit, beacon masts, handles, or tool joints;
- exposed bolts, seams, clamps, welded patches, cable runs, and service labels;
- dark graphite interiors, off-white worn hull exteriors, and muted orange survival/interact accents;
- asymmetry caused by crash damage, field repairs, and salvaged replacement parts.

### Forbidden Human Forms

- elegant spaceship silhouettes;
- smooth capsule hulls;
- toy-like proportions;
- random cubes without manufacturing logic;
- paper-thin panels;
- glossy sci-fi plastic;
- excessive glow strips;
- forms that look like board-game pieces or construction blocks.

## Alien / Galaxabrain Technology

Alien and Galaxabrain tech must read as dark biomechanical, asymmetrical, hostile, organic-mechanical, and locally energized.

### Required Alien Qualities

- broken symmetry with one dominant threat read;
- dark shell, tendon, cable, sinew, or exposed mechanical-organic transitions;
- cyan/turquoise localized glow in eyes, organs, weak points, seams, or power nodes;
- sharp or hooked threat accents used sparingly;
- silhouettes that are readable as hostile at distance.

### Forbidden Alien Qualities

- cute mascot proportions;
- cartoon monster shapes;
- generic robot shapes;
- clean human-manufactured panel logic;
- full-body neon glow;
- random spikes without anatomy or mechanical purpose.

## Volcanic Terrain

Volcanic terrain must feel like irregular basalt and ash shaped by crash impact, not like platforms or panels.

### Required Terrain Qualities

- irregular basalt masses with broken side faces;
- ash blends and rough deposits around rocks and wreckage;
- impact basin shapes with raised rims and fractured surfaces;
- foreground, midground, and background depth layers;
- silhouettes that guide movement through massing, value, and negative space rather than slabs.

### Forbidden Terrain Qualities

- platform or board read;
- flat rectangular islands;
- panel ridges;
- pyramid ridges;
- pure black void ground;
- route slabs, cards, ribbons, or artificial strips;
- terrain that repeats the same ridge module without erosion, fracture, or scale variation.
