# TitanCraft Material Palette

## Palette Intent

Materials must support Polygonal Salvage Sci-Fi: grounded, worn, repaired, readable, and simplified. Materials should not chase photorealism; they should expose function and wear through clear material groups.

## Human Material Families

| Family | Target read | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Worn off-white hull | crashed manufactured exterior | chipped paint, dust, scorch marks, exposed darker underlayer |
| Dark graphite interior | cabin, machinery, broken structure | matte to satin, high readability against hull exterior |
| Muted orange accent | survival, interactable, caution | used for handles, latches, rescue marks, active affordances |
| Bare steel | bolts, brackets, worn edges | rough, scratched, not mirror chrome |
| Rubber and cable black | hoses, conduit, grip | matte, practical, not decorative |
| Dust and ash overlay | crash-site integration | localized grounding, bottom-up dirt, impact staining |

## Alien / Galaxabrain Material Families

| Family | Target read | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Dark biomechanical shell | hostile alien surface | charcoal, desaturated blue-black, bruised dark purple, or oil-dark green |
| Wet-organic accent | limited tissue/mechanical seams | controlled roughness variation, not gore-heavy |
| Exposed metal-organic joint | hard/soft transition | broken cables, tendons, braces, embedded plates |
| Cyan/turquoise glow | energy, sensor, weak point, threat read | localized only; must never flood the whole body |

## Terrain Material Families

| Family | Target read | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Basalt grey | volcanic rock mass | fractured, angular, mid-to-dark grey, never pure black void |
| Ash blend | walkable dust and deposits | soft value shifts, not flat cards |
| Scorch and impact marks | crash energy | radial, directional, or contact-based around wreckage |
| Distant ridge haze | background depth | lower contrast than foreground, not black silhouettes only |

## Glow Rules

Glow is selective. It may identify alien power, an interact prompt, a beacon state, or a weak point. It must not be used as decoration across every edge, panel, cable, or terrain ridge.

## Automatic Material Rejections

- glossy plastic hulls;
- bright debug colors left in final review;
- random material slots with no naming or purpose;
- full-surface neon emission;
- pure black terrain without ash, basalt, or readable side faces;
- photoreal texture noise that destroys polygonal readability.
