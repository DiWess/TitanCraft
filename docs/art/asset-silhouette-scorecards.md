# Asset Silhouette Scorecards

Each future Blender Asset Forge production asset must pass its relevant scorecard before material approval. Required camera angles apply to neutral-grey review and material review unless noted.

## Heavy Crashed Hull

- Two-second readability target: heavy damaged human spacecraft hull section, not a toy spaceship.
- Required silhouette features: rectangular mass, thick panels, broken ribs, exposed interior voids, asymmetric crash damage, grounded contact points.
- Forbidden silhouette features: smooth capsule body, elegant yacht profile, paper-thin shells, random cubes, excessive fins.
- Neutral-grey review criteria: mass reads without color; damage breaks symmetry; panel thickness is visible.
- Material-pass criteria: off-white worn hull, dark graphite interior, muted orange survival marks, scorch and ash grounding.
- Automatic rejection triggers: toy proportions, glossy plastic read, no scale cues, decorative glow strips.
- Required camera angles: front three-quarter, side silhouette, top-down massing, low ground-level scale.

## Volcanic Impact Basin

- Two-second readability target: volcanic crash basin with raised fractured rim.
- Required silhouette features: irregular rim, basin depression, broken side faces, basalt chunks, ash transitions, depth layers.
- Forbidden silhouette features: rectangular island, platform slab, route ribbon, pyramid ridge grid, pure black void floor.
- Neutral-grey review criteria: basin and rim read without materials; route is implied by mass and value, not cards.
- Material-pass criteria: basalt grey variation, ash blends, scorch direction, non-black shadow detail.
- Automatic rejection triggers: flat board read, panel ridges, no foreground/midground/background depth.
- Required camera angles: player eye-level approach, top-down route read, side cross-section, background ridge framing.

## Background Ridge Family

- Two-second readability target: distant volcanic ridges framing the crash site.
- Required silhouette features: irregular ridgeline, scale variation, broken basalt forms, lower contrast than foreground.
- Forbidden silhouette features: repeated teeth, pyramid rows, panel seams, equal-height walls, black cutouts.
- Neutral-grey review criteria: ridges support depth and direction; repetition is not obvious.
- Material-pass criteria: desaturated basalt, atmospheric value falloff, ash and dust softening.
- Automatic rejection triggers: copied ridge modules, artificial wall read, route-blocking slab read.
- Required camera angles: horizon framing, player eye-level, side silhouette, wide establishing view.

## Industrial Workbench

- Two-second readability target: practical salvaged crafting station.
- Required silhouette features: rectangular bench mass, tool surface, clamps, storage modules, cables, bolted legs, one interact side.
- Forbidden silhouette features: magic altar, decorative console, toy table, random cube pile, paper-thin work surface.
- Neutral-grey review criteria: usable surface and interaction side read without color.
- Material-pass criteria: graphite worktop, worn metal frame, off-white salvaged panels, muted orange interact accent.
- Automatic rejection triggers: no functional surface, excessive glow, clean luxury sci-fi read.
- Required camera angles: front interact view, side profile, top work surface, player-height close-up.

## Save Point

- Two-second readability target: safe local checkpoint device built from crash salvage.
- Required silhouette features: anchored base, vertical readable marker, protective casing, cable or battery module, calm state indicator.
- Forbidden silhouette features: fantasy shrine, floating orb, random glowing cube, elegant capsule, oversized UI prop.
- Neutral-grey review criteria: anchor and marker read from player distance.
- Material-pass criteria: dark graphite base, off-white repaired casing, muted orange or soft status accent, minimal glow.
- Automatic rejection triggers: full neon body, unclear function, too similar to beacon.
- Required camera angles: approach view, side silhouette, top footprint, close interaction angle.

## Beacon

- Two-second readability target: emergency rescue beacon objective.
- Required silhouette features: vertical mast or dish, rugged base, protected lens, cables, deployable or repaired modules.
- Forbidden silhouette features: complete rocket, elegant antenna sculpture, toy traffic cone, magic crystal, excessive glow tower.
- Neutral-grey review criteria: objective importance reads through height and mast/dish shape.
- Material-pass criteria: worn off-white modules, graphite mechanical base, muted orange rescue markings, controlled lens glow.
- Automatic rejection triggers: rocket implication, decorative neon, no industrial support structure.
- Required camera angles: distance objective view, front interact view, side mast read, low heroic angle.

## Resource Pickup

- Two-second readability target: collectible metal, biomasse, or electronics component tied to MVP resources.
- Required silhouette features: hand-scale chunk, clear resource family shape, pickup outline, grounded placement.
- Forbidden silhouette features: generic colored cube, loot gem, coin, candy shape, unreadable debris speck.
- Neutral-grey review criteria: silhouette differentiates resource family before color.
- Material-pass criteria: metal is worn steel, biomasse is organic-mechanical residue, electronics show circuit fragments or modules.
- Automatic rejection triggers: debug-color dependency, toy pickup, no scale cue.
- Required camera angles: ground approach, top-down pickup read, side profile, close inventory-style view.

## Galaxabrain Scout

- Two-second readability target: hostile dark biomechanical scout enemy.
- Required silhouette features: asymmetric body, one dominant sensor/threat read, organic-mechanical limbs or appendages, low predatory mass.
- Forbidden silhouette features: cute monster, generic robot, humanoid soldier, smooth mascot head, symmetrical clean drone.
- Neutral-grey review criteria: hostile intent and body direction read without glow.
- Material-pass criteria: dark biomechanical shell, controlled wet/metal transitions, localized cyan/turquoise glow.
- Automatic rejection triggers: full neon body, toy proportions, generic robot paneling, friendly expression.
- Required camera angles: front threat view, side movement silhouette, top-down combat read, damaged/weak-point material view.

## First-Person Mechanical Arm

- Two-second readability target: salvaged mechanical arm upgrade built from human crash technology.
- Required silhouette features: forearm mass, piston or hinge cylinders, rectangular armor plates, exposed bolts/cables, practical hand/tool end.
- Forbidden silhouette features: sleek superhero gauntlet, fantasy claw, random block arm, glossy toy plastic, excessive glow.
- Neutral-grey review criteria: arm function and first-person readability remain clear at screen edge.
- Material-pass criteria: worn metal, graphite joints, off-white repaired panels, muted orange interact accents, dirt and edge wear.
- Automatic rejection triggers: blocks too much screen, no mechanical logic, hand/tool silhouette unclear.
- Required camera angles: first-person idle, first-person attack/use pose, side orthographic, close material pass.
